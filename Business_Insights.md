# Business Insights

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and overall business revenue using SQL.

---

## Key Business Insights

### Highest Revenue City

Coimbatore generated the highest revenue among all cities.

---

### Highest Spending Customers

Customers were ranked based on total revenue using SQL Window Functions.

---

### Product Performance

Electronics products generated the highest sales revenue.

---

### Customer Revenue Analysis

Customers generating more than ₹40,000 revenue were identified using GROUP BY and HAVING clauses.

---

### Above Average Customers

Customers whose spending exceeded the average customer revenue were identified using SQL Subqueries.

---

### Advanced SQL

CTEs and Window Functions were used to simplify complex queries and rank customers based on revenue.s
